A Q&A On Embroidery


What is this hobby exactly?

Mixed-media hand embroidery is mixing hand embroidery with at least one other element such as paint, beads, or other objects.

How does it make you feel?

How it makes me feel depends on the nature of the piece I am working on. Usually my feelings come first and then I create but there are times the opposite is true. I do my best art when I am in a good mood and working on a piece where the subject is close to my heart. When I'm creating while upset, all I can see is what's gone wrong so I tend to stay away at those times. I do enjoy what I call "mindless paintings" when I'm feeling down though. I just apply bright paint to a canvas and where ever it goes, it stays.

What experiences has your hobby provided, that you might not otherwise experience?

Creating art of my favorite wrestlers and tv characters has given me the opportunity to meet so many wonderful people who also enjoy the same things I do. It's also provided me a way to show my appreciation to those who inspire me in a way they can see and interact with.
